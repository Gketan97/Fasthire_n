FAQ's

What is the FastHire Referral Program?

The Fasthire program helps candidates find a referral in their target company in minimum time duration. You need to fill a form in the apply section and wait for further communication from our team. In the form, you need to fill in basic details,resume and job description for target role.

Is there any fee for the referral program?

The FastHire referral program is completely free.

How many referral applications can I apply for?

You can apply for a referral in one company only once in 6 Months. Our platform will automatically filter out duplicate applications.

Will I get shortlisted for an interview after applying?

No,a referral application is not a confirmation for an interview call. Only profiles relevant to the job opening will be shared further. But, the profiles shared by us have a good probability of receiving an interview call from a recruiter.

Why should I refer candidates from the FastHire platform?

The job-hunting process is emotional and comes with plenty of ups and down.We are building a community which will help candidates to go through job hunt phase without hassle and to connect with professionals in their target companies.

For which roles can I find referrals through the FastHire Referral Program?

Currently ,We are focussed on Technology and Data Science related roles.We will soon be expanding our help in other job roles.
